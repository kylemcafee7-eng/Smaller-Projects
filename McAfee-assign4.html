What is Grunge?

Grunge, literally meaning grimy or dirty in the word's common use,
is a type of rock music characterized by "raucous guitar sound and lazy vocal delivery".
Or at least this is the definition you'll get from bing. Personally, I resent the word "lazy"
in describing Grunge. Grunge is visceral, taking a lot of inspiration from metal and hard rock with vocals
that have a lot of range. There is often a slower drawl to many prominent songs,
but there's also strained screams, voice cracking bleats and low croaks.
The guitar as well is not always raucous and can often be soft and melodic, though
typically in a way that would sound off to your average follower of music theory.
To me grunge is the genre that best characterizes pain, and there's something very honest about that.

My Favorite Grunge Songs:

but having a notable melodic undertone.
"Jeremy"
A dark warning about a child who shot himself in class.
"Nutshell"
Though there are some coarse vocals, this song is perhaps the most melodic
and morose song in the generally intense genre of grunge.
